about me

I'm a backend software developer with a background in physics and education. Before becoming a developer, I contributed to the LIGO scientific community as a thin film optics researcher and developed curriculum as a high school physics teacher. Every day, I wake ready to grapple with novel problems. Finding elegant solutions instills in me a sense of wonder. I'm particularly interested in software that makes privacy and encryption accessible to everyone, everywhere.
